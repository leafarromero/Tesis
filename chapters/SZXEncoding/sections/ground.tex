\subsection{The ZX-Ground extension}
Multiple extensions to the ZX-calculus have been proposed. In this work, we will
deal with the grounded and scalable extensions. We first present the base calculus
with the grounded-ZX extension, denoted \zxGND~\cite{ground}, to allow us to encode
quantum state measurement operations.

In conjunction to the previously described generators, this extension includes a
new one, the $\ground$ (ground). This generator characterizes the act of discarding
a qubit, in the semantics this is represented via the partial trace operation.

When combining the ground with a 3-degree red or green spider, it corresponds
to a measurement on the computational or Hadamard basis respectively.
